Old electronics don't belong in the trash. They contain valuable materials that can be recovered and hazardous substances that need proper handling.

We're working on establishing a proper e-waste recycling program for the Puerto Morelos area. In the meantime, contact us and we can discuss options for your electronics.

Data Security

Before recycling any device, we can securely wipe all data to protect your privacy. For devices that can't be wiped, we can physically destroy the storage media.
