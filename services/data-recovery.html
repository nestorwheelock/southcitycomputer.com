Lost important files? Accidentally deleted photos? Hard drive making clicking sounds? We specialize in recovering data from failed, damaged, or corrupted storage devices.

Using professional-grade recovery tools and techniques, we've successfully recovered data from drives that other shops said were hopeless. We offer a "no data, no charge" policy on standard recoveries.

Important: What NOT To Do

  • Don't keep using the device — Every write operation decreases recovery chances
  • Don't run disk repair tools — They can overwrite recoverable data
  • Don't open a hard drive — Dust particles can destroy platters
  • Don't freeze your hard drive — This myth causes more damage
